Welcome To CCHS

“Council of Community Health Service” (CCHS), which is located at Dallu, Swoyambhu was established in 1998 A.D. CCHS is a community hospital that provides medical services and healthcare to people at affordable cost and has up-to-date medical equipment’s. They provide wide range of facilities such as OPD, pathology tests, vaccination, physiotherapy, pharmaceutical, etc. As they are community hospital they conduct various heath campaign, health education programs, vaccination and other wellness programs.
